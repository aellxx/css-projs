LOCATIONS
Super Matcha Seongsu
The flagship store, Super Matcha Seongsu, has a modern and understated space design in collaboration with Studio Unlabel. It is designed with granite and other materials that can capture the young and dynamic contents of Super Matcha. In particular, MALO, the world's first whisking matcha robot, suggests a new appearance as a space where past culture and future technology overlap. It is also a space to expand new content and image of Super Matcha through collaboration with various category brands.
19, Seoul Forest 6-gil, Seongdong-gu, Seoul
Super Matcha Lotte World Mall
Super Matcha Lotte World Mall is located on the B1 floor of Lotte World Mall, a landmark mall in the center of Seoul. It is in an easily accessible location in the middle of many cities so people of all ages can stop. Since our signature ice cream, The Tea Cone is always on sale, you can enjoy the sweetness in the indoor space regardless of the season. It is also one of the stores that many people are looking for as a coffee alternative as it goes well with nearby dessert and bakery brands.
300 Olympic-ro, Songpa-gu, Seoul, Lotte World Mall B1
Super Matcha The Hyundai Seoul
Super Matcha The Hyundai, which is the store that has entered along with the opening of The Hyundai Seoul, is located on the B1 floor with famous F&B stores. Although it is small and for takeouts only, it is a representative antenna store that attracts the most matcha lovers and new visitors. Super Matcha The Hyundai has its own custom bottle recipe menu, offering differentiated content that makes beverages suit individual tastes.
108 Yeouido-daero, Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul
Super Matcha Dongtan
As the first Super Matcha store in Gyeonggi-do, we entered with the opening of the Lotte Department Store Dongtan, the largest department store in Gyeonggi-do. Super Matcha Dongtan is in the form of a dining zone on the B1 floor. It operates a variety of MD products and RTD drinks that even children can drink in consideration of the characteristics of customers visiting the department store. It is also one of the stores with many regular customers due to its spacious and pleasant atmosphere.
160 Dongtan Station-ro, Hwaseong-si, Gyeonggi-do
Super Matcha THE CAFÉ
Super Matcha The Café Mok-dong, located in the restaurant area of Hyundai Department Store Mok-dong, opened under the name of The Café, which combines a bakery. To expand the value of being a superfood from a brand where health and fashion can coexist, we are introducing a bakery using healthy ingredients for the first time. We also have secured customers of various age groups.
2576th floor, Mokdong-ro, Yangcheon-gu, Seoul
